*javax.xml.bind.annotation.adapters.CollapsedStringAdapter_Description*

Built-in (|javax.xml.bind.annotation.adapters.XmlAdapter|) to handle xs:token and its derived types. 

This adapter removes leading and trailing whitespaces, then truncate any sequnce of tab, CR, LF, and SP by a single whitespace 
character ' '.
