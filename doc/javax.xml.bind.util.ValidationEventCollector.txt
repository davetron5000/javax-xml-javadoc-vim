*javax.xml.bind.util.ValidationEventCollector_Description*

ValidationEventHandler(|javax.xml.bind.ValidationEventHandler|) implementation that collects all events. 

To use this class, create a new instance and pass it to the setEventHandler method of the Validator, Unmarshaller, Marshaller 
class. After the call to validate or unmarshal completes, call the getEvents method to retrieve all the reported errors and 
warnings.
