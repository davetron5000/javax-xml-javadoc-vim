*javax.xml.crypto.URIDereferencer_Description*

A dereferencer of (|javax.xml.crypto.URIReference|) s. 

The result of dereferencing a URIReference is either an instance of (|javax.xml.crypto.OctetStreamData|) or 
(|javax.xml.crypto.NodeSetData|) . Unless the URIReference is a same-document reference as defined in section 4.2 of the W3C 
Recommendation for XML-Signature Syntax and Processing, the result of dereferencing the URIReference MUST be an 
OctetStreamData.
